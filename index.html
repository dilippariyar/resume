About Me

I am a passionate professional specializing in **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)** and **web accessibility**, holding a Bachelor of Management Studies in Marketing. My comprehensive SEO expertise covers **on-page, off-page, and technical aspects**—from optimizing crucial elements like **meta tags, Open Graph, Twitter Cards, and Schema markup** for rich results, to managing `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, and **Google Search Console**. Crucially, I am dedicated to crafting truly inclusive digital experiences by rigorously applying **Semantic HTML, ARIA, and WCAG 2.2 principles** (**Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust**), ensuring seamless navigation and understanding for all users, including those relying on screen readers like **NVDA** and **TalkBack**.
